Stray

Stray, short for Strategic Payment, is an online banking platform designed to provide users with a safe and intuitive experience in managing their bank accounts. With both a website and app version, Stray emphasizes responsive design to accommodate users across various devices and locations. The platform's design is inspired by the game “Stray”. This creates a playful and futuristic feel, recognizing the importance of an appealing and modern interface for user comfort and satisfaction (and contributes to a fun experience for the designer :D).

Setting up the Project

The goal of this project was to create an intuitive website and an app version of a finance platform, but based on something I like, whether its a book, movie, game or any other hobby. This would help me to apply a playful touch to my design without ruining the user experience. I chose the video game Stray for its futuristic look and started creating personas, the user flow and LoFi wireframes. After that, I built my design system consisting of atoms, molecules and organisms, step by step.

Onboarding

In the crucial onboarding phase, the focus is on creating a sense of familiarity for users. The aesthetic embraces a futuristic look, aiming to not only simplify the user experience but also establish a welcoming atmosphere from the very start of their interaction with the platform.

Compact Menus

Both the app and website versions feature collapsible menus for a compact interface. The icon designs are self-explanatory and intuitively organized, ensuring logical navigation that enhances user understanding and accessibility.

Card Overview

The card overview page is designed to provide users with instant access to essential information. It showcases a slideshow of all the user's cards, along with corresponding spendings and earnings details. Users can quickly grasp their financial activity during a specific period or by categories, ensuring a comprehensive overview at first glance.

Transaction History

The transaction history displays a list of previous transactions. Users can easily switch between cards and view corresponding transactions. Repeating transfers are prioritized at the top, followed by one-time transactions listed chronologically. Each transaction entry includes the most crucial information, such as, for example, color-coded indicators for spendings or earnings, ensuring a quick access of information.

Intuitive Transactions

Recognizing that sending money can be intimidating for users, the process is meticulously designed to evoke a sense of security. The transaction inputs are straightforward, descriptive, and logically ordered, aiming to eliminate any ambiguity. By ensuring simplicity and completeness, users are guided through the process seamlessly, alleviating concerns and fostering a sense of safety.

Secure Confirmation

After entering all transaction details, users are prompted for a code sent to their phone, reinforcing the sense of security. Confirmation of this code is mandatory for transaction completion, ensuring an additional layer of safety. Users retain the option to cancel the transaction if they feel uneasy. Extra confirmation screens provide clear feedback on the success or cancellation of the transaction, enhancing user comprehension of the process and system status.
