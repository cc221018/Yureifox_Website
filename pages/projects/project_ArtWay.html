ArtWay

ArtWay is a website which enables people to dive deeper into the world of art and gain and share valuable knowledge about the art industry and its professional artists. In the end, it aims to helps young aspiring artists to have a much better chance at making a career as an artist or animator of any category. The finished product is a result of the final 2 project weeks of my second semester, also known as the “Creative CodeLabs 2”.

LoFi Designs

The Creative CodeLabs (CCL) occur at the semester's end, requiring me to apply all the knowledge acquired throughout my courses to craft a final product. This entails donning the hats of a designer, front-end developer, and back-end developer. To manage time effectively, I streamlined the design phase, focusing solely on finalizing the LoFi prototype. Although a condensed approach, it laid a solid foundation for the project.

HiFi Test Designs

After finishing the LoFi wireframes, I delved into testing colors and crafting a logo. With a vision of creating a welcoming space for artists, the website was designed to exude an artistic touch. Employing vibrant, lighter colors to symbolize a bright future empowered by the platform felt like the perfect fit - I was ready to start the coding phase of my project.

Onboarding

The onboarding phase aims to inspire and welcome users, incorporating an artistic touch. It features the same vibrant colors as the rest of the website for a consistent and inviting experience.

The 3 Main Pages

ArtWay facilitates the swift retrieval of valuable information for young artists through three main pages: Insights, Artworks, and Artists. Each category serves as a dedicated space to streamline and organize information for a more efficient user experience: Artist profiles, their artworks and their insight posts. Similar to Tweets or blog posts, ArtWay’s Insights emphasize textual content over visuals. These posts, encompassing artists' comments, stories, and more, offer valuable tips and explanations on diverse topics within the art industry.

Customizable User Profile

On a user's - or artist's - profile, essential information such as country, status, and name is showcased. Two lists containing all their posts, including artworks and insight posts, are prominently displayed. Users have the flexibility to customize and edit both private and public information on their own profile page, ranging from profile picture, status, and posts to email and password. This allows for a personalized ArtWay experience tailored to individual preferences.

Simplistic Posting

Users can effortlessly share their artistic creations or insights on ArtWay through a straightforward process. Simple input fields, easy picture uploading, and clear buttons for switching between artwork and insight posts contribute to a user-friendly experience.
